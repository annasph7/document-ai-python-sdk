Our **API Documentation** is available online via https://app.konfuzio.com/api. Have a look at our YouTube_API_Tutorial_ too.

The Konfuzio Software Development Kit, the **Konfuzio SDK**, can be installed via pip_install_konfuzio-sdk_. Find examples in Python and review the source code on GitHub_.

In addition, enterprise clients do have access to our Python_Konfuzio_Trainer_Module_ to define, train and run custom Document AI.

Download the **On-Prem and Private Cloud** documentation to deploy Konfuzio on a single VM or on your cluster. Use this_link_ to access the latest version.
